Thesis Information

  • Title: Hybrid Representation for Neural and Parametric 3D Animal Model
  • Student: Paulína Jančárová
  • Supervisor: Mgr. Martin Halaj
  • Consultant: doc. RNDr. Martin Madaras, PhD.
  • Description: This thesis investigates hybrid approaches to the representation and rendering of 3D models by combining traditional parametric polygonal meshes with neural representations such as Neural Radiance Fields (NeRFs) or 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS). The goal is to design a hybrid representation of a 3D animal model, where the body is modeled using a parametric mesh and the fur is represented using one of the aforementioned neural representations.
